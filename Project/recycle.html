Why you should Recycle!

Recycling has many benefits towards our enviroment and habitats. If we are to recycle more often

  • We would save more resources, prevent pollution, create jobs, saves money, and saves the enviroment.

  • Recycling also has some benefits as well, such as saving energy. If we are to recycle about 30% per year we would save a tremendous amount of gasoline and reduce greenhouse gases.

  • Recycling also prevents pollution. If we are to recycle half of the stuff that we use, we can save resources, while also avoiding enviromental damages caused by harvesting of earths resources.
