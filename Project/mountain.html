Bath County, VA

The hotsprings is a beautiful place to visit! It is a hotsprings way up in the mountains of bath county. Unfortunately it is closed off to the public, as it is now considered private property. It's now covered in tons of trees so it can be kinda hard to see the beauty of the hotsprings. However, this is one of the most prettiest places I've been too. The water was crystal clear and almost see through! If it to ever open up again, I reccomend anyone to visit this place.
- GKF

Blue Ridge Mountains, VA

This picture was taken along walking at Camp Blue Ridge. However, I've hiked up a few places in the blue ridge mountains and I have to say that, The blue Ridge Mountains have a variety of beautiful views overlooking counties, cities and and more mountains. If you ever have the time to ride through the blue ridge parkway or even hike one of the trails in the Blue Ridge Mountains, You'll won't regret it!
- GKF
