Outerbanks, NC

This is one of my most favorite beach spots. The outerbanks at Nagshead NC. It is a small little peninsula on the outskirts of NC that is famous for it's beaches, fishing spots, and food!

Myrtle Beach, SC

Myrtle Beach is one of those beaches you've been to and probably won't ever forget. The beaches are very calm at night. Myrtle Beach is the beach you would want to go to if you enjoy calm wave sounds.
