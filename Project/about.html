About Beauflor


Beauflors Goals and purpose

Beauflors Purpose is to show off the best sceneries that there are to visit using the creator of this website images and personal reccomendations.
Beauflors goal is to give informative information on the benefits of recycling while also raising money for
local organizations that help natural habitats and local comunities.
Our aim is to attract visitors whom love nature or simply
want to visit someplace new, while also giving them informative information.
